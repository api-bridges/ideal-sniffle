FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

01
What happens after the free month?
After your free first month, you'll be moved to the Starter plan at $49/month unless you upgrade to Pro. We'll email you a reminder before the trial ends. No credit card is required to start — you only need to add payment details when you're ready to continue.
02
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, completely. There are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
03
What's included in all plans?
Every plan — including the free trial — includes full access to all 500+ APIs across all 9 categories with zero rate limits. There are no tiered model access restrictions; all plans get the same model library.
04
Do you have a startup discount?
Yes. We offer a 50% discount for early-stage startups (pre-Series A). Contact us at enterprise@getsetgoapi.com with your company details and we'll set you up with a discounted plan. We believe in supporting the builder ecosystem.
05
Is there usage-based pricing?
No. All our plans are flat-rate monthly subscriptions with no usage caps and no per-call fees. We believe predictable pricing makes building easier, so we've deliberately avoided the complexity of token-based or call-count billing.
