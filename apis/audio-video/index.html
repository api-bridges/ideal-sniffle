Available APIs

40+ Audio & Video APIs

01
OpenAI Whisper — Speech to Text
OpenAI's open-source speech recognition model supporting 100+ languages. Industry-leading transcription accuracy for accents, technical vocabulary, and noisy environments. Returns timestamps, speaker diarisation, and language detection.
02
ElevenLabs — AI Voice Synthesis
The gold standard for AI-generated speech. Extraordinarily realistic voices in 30+ languages, emotional voice modulation, voice cloning from 30 seconds of audio, and a library of 1000+ pre-made voices.
03
AssemblyAI — Audio Intelligence
Enterprise speech-to-text with built-in intelligence layers: automatic speaker diarisation, PII redaction, sentiment analysis, auto-chapters, and topic detection — all from a single audio file API call.
04
Deepgram — Real-Time Transcription
Streaming speech-to-text API optimised for real-time applications. Sub-300ms latency on live audio, custom model training on domain vocabulary, and WebSocket streaming for live transcription in production.
05
Runway ML & Pika Labs — AI Video
Generate cinematic video from text prompts or still images. Runway Gen-2 and Pika 1.0 create 3-4 second HD video clips with temporal consistency. Ideal for ad creatives, product demos, and artistic projects.
06
Voice Cloning APIs
Create custom voice models from audio samples. ElevenLabs, PlayHT, and Resemble AI voice cloning APIs let you build personalised TTS experiences, dub content into new voices, or create consistent brand voices.
